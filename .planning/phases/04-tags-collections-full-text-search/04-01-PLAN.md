<objective>
Deliver the Tags vertical slice through ORG-01: a user can attach/detach tags to their notes by name (auto-create-on-attach, normalized, per-user) and list their tags. This plan also lays the two pieces of shared schema foundation the rest of Phase 4 builds on: the `Note.tags` relationship + `selectinload` (so every Note-returning query keeps working once `NoteRead` gains a `tags` field) and the FULLTEXT index rebuilt at `min_token_size=2` (consumed by the search slice).

Purpose: Tags are the first organization layer and the slice that forces the `selectinload` + schema changes every later slice depends on.
Output: `app/tags/` domain package, migration `0004`, extended `Note`/`NoteRead`/`NoteRepository`, registered router, and green `tests/test_tags.py`.
</objective>
